OUR MISSION

  • To focus on value-based education that instils honesty, integrity, compassion and mutual respect.
  • To encourage to make a difference to the world by instilling a sense of personal accountability and responsibility towards others and a commitment to charitable endeavours.
  • To develop outstanding Indian citizens who are proud of, and contribute to, the social and economic prosperity of our country.
  • To provide a high quality and intellectually challenging educational environment which instils high aspirations, a desire to learn and ambition to achieve.
  • To provide a passionate and personalised approach to secure excellence, stemming from a passionate belief that each individual is unique and special and capable of rising above any perceived limitations.

OUR CORE VALUES

In pursuit of Knowledge with
  • Respect – Treating others as we wish to be treated.
  • Service – Serving our community and the world at large.
  • Excellence – Excelling in everything we do.
  • Teamwork – Working together to achieve excellence.
